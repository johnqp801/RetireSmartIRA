## What's actually in 2.0.1

1. **OBBBA senior-bonus itemization fix** (correctness; merged to main 2026-07-06 as `f88966b`/`32db2de` BEFORE this feature branch): a 65+ filer who itemizes and is under the MAGI phaseout was losing the $6,000/$12,000 senior deduction. The deduction now applies whether the filer takes the standard deduction or itemizes. This fix was NOT in the shipped 2.0.0 (Mac 57 / iOS 58) and is the reason 2.0.1 exists.
2. **Senior Bonus card on Tax Summary** (visibility): `SeniorBonusDeductionCard()` now renders on `DashboardView` (Tax Summary), both compact and wide layouts. Self-hides for under-65. Previously only on Scenarios → Charitable Contributions.
3. **Per-chart "Explain this chart" popovers** (feature): deterministic `ChartCommentary` on all five Multi-Year chart models + reusable `ChartInfoButton` (ⓘ → popover) wired into every chart header. Offline, adapts to the user's numbers, no LLM.
