# 1.8.2 Release Notes — Three Options

Drafted: 2026-05-17 — at the end of Phase 3 (the 1.8.2 release cut).

The 1.8.2 release ships Phase 1 (Ron-segment polish), Phase 2 (analyst-critique
additions), and Phase 3 (higher-earner additions + housekeeping). These options
focus on Phase 3 highlights for the App Store-facing notes; expand or recombine
with Phase 1/2 items as desired.

---

## Option A — Feature-led, neutral

**Title:** Higher-earner refinements and cliff awareness

**Body:**
- NIIT cliff awareness: a new approaching/triggered band on the NIIT chart shows when your scenario is closing in on the 3.8% net-investment surtax threshold.
- SS Tax Torpedo warning: when your provisional income lands in the Social Security taxability phase-in, Scenarios now flags the elevated effective marginal rate.
- OBBBA Senior Bonus prominence: the age-65+ deduction (+6K per person, +12K MFJ both 65+) is now surfaced as a named line in the deduction breakdown, with live phase-out detection.
- Behind the scenes: 2026 tax constants refreshed against IRS, SSA, HHS, and CMS published values; OBBBA phase-out math aligned with IRC 151(d)(5) per-individual structure.

---

## Option B — Strategy-led

**Title:** Sharper signals for high-income retirement planning

**Body:**
RetireSmartIRA 1.8.2 sharpens the signals that matter most for higher-income retirees. The NIIT chart now warns as you approach the 3.8% surtax threshold. The Social Security Tax Torpedo — a known gotcha where each new dollar of income can be taxed at an effective ~40% — is now surfaced inline in Scenarios. The OBBBA Senior Bonus deduction (age 65+) is now a named line in your deduction breakdown so you can see exactly how much it's saving you and when phase-out kicks in.

We also refreshed every 2026 tax constant against the latest IRS, SSA, HHS, and CMS publications, with regression tests pinning each one so they stay accurate.
