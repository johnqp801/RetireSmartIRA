## Disagreement resolution — Missouri

ChatGPT flagged the MO thresholds as TY 2025 values, not TY 2026. Direct
verification against MO DOR 2026 Withholding Tax Formula PDF (page 13,
Annual Payroll column) confirmed ChatGPT was correct.

- Source of error: MO DOR "year-changes" page publishes the schedule for
  the most recent FILED tax year (TY 2025, for returns filed April 2026).
  The agent that originally researched MO read that page and reported its
  values as TY 2026.
- Correct TY 2026 values (inflation-bumped +2.67% per MO § 143.011):
  $0 / $1,348 / $2,696 / $4,044 / $5,392 / $6,740 / $8,088 / $9,436
- Engine corrected in commit `897d7ef`.

This is exactly the value TriSTAR source #4 provides — an independent
critical reader. Gemini matched what was prominent on the DOR site;
ChatGPT triangulated against the withholding formula PDF and caught
the year mislabel.
