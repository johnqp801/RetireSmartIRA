### Bracket-engine semantics

`B(threshold: X, rate: Y)` is a list of bracket-start points. Income in the range [X_n, X_{n+1}) is taxed at rate Y_n. A `B(threshold: 0, rate: 0.0)` is an explicit zero-bracket. Same-array brackets are used for filing statuses that share the schedule (DE, RI, MO, DC, etc.).
