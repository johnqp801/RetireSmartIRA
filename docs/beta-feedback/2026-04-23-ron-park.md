## Withdrawal order — Brokerage → Trad → Roth

- `[DISCUSS]` Ron's question: "Is there ever an advantage to withdraw from IRA before brokerage?" Yes, in a few cases:
  - Low-income year (fill low brackets with traditional withdrawals to avoid RMD spike later).
  - Pre-65 ACA subsidy management (limit capital-gains realization).
  - Estate planning (brokerage step-up basis favors preserving brokerage to heirs).
- `[ROADMAP]` Proper withdrawal-order *strategy* guidance requires modeling brokerage accounts. Helper text is a near-term fix; full strategy comparison is a bigger feature.
